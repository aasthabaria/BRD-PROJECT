# RetailPulse – Business Requirements & Data Automation Project

## Overview
RetailPulse is a business analysis project addressing a real-world problem: fragmented sales and inventory data spread across multiple databases within a retail organization. The goal was to define clear business requirements, identify automation opportunities, and deliver actionable insights through structured documentation and data analysis.

## Business Problem
The company lacked a unified view of its sales data. Teams were manually pulling reports from separate databases, leading to inconsistencies, delays, and poor decision-making. This project aimed to automate data consolidation and surface meaningful insights through structured analysis.

## What I Did
- **Stakeholder Identification & Interviews** – Mapped key stakeholders across operations, finance, and IT; conducted requirements elicitation sessions to capture pain points and priorities
- **Business Requirements Document (BRD)** – Authored a full BRD defining scope, functional and non-functional requirements, assumptions, and constraints
- **SQL Queries** – Wrote SQL queries to extract, join, and validate data across multiple tables and databases
- **Data Visualization** – Built supporting visuals to communicate findings and trends to non-technical stakeholders

## Tools & Skills Used
- SQL (data extraction, joins, aggregations)
- Microsoft Excel (data validation, pivot analysis)
- Power BI (visualization)
- BRD documentation methodology (IIBA standards)
